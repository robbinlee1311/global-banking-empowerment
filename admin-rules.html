Rules & Audit

Role rules

User role
  • Can create an account and sign in as a customer.
  • Can view only their own balances, cards, transfers, statements, and profile.
  • Can initiate transfers only from available checking balance.
  • Cannot approve accounts, view all customers, override holds, or change risk rules.
Admin role
  • Can review customers, KYC state, risk status, and pending approvals.
  • Can monitor transfers, flag suspicious activity, and place administrative holds.
  • Can see operational dashboards and audit controls.
  • Cannot move money as a customer from the admin portal.
