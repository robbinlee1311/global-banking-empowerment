Privacy policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, phone number, address, and financial information when you open an account or use our services.

We also collect information automatically, including device information, IP address, browser type, and usage data through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our banking services
  • Process transactions and send notifications
  • Detect and prevent fraud and security incidents
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our products and services

3. Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Financial partners for processing transactions
  • Regulatory authorities when required by law
  • Law enforcement in response to valid legal requests

4. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures including 256-bit AES encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits to protect your information.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of certain data collection and marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@globalbankingempowerment.com.
